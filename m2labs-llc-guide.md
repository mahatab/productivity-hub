## 📋 Complete Checklist

### Phase 1: Pre-Registration
- [ ] **Choose LLC name** - Verify "m2labs LLC" is available
  - Search: [Washington Secretary of State Business Search](https://www.sos.wa.gov/corps/search.aspx)
  - Must include "LLC" or "Limited Liability Company"
  
- [ ] **Choose Registered Agent**
  - Must have physical WA address
  - Can be you or hire a service ($100-300/year)
  - Options: Northwest Registered Agent, Incfile, LegalZoom

### Phase 2: State Registration (Washington)
- [ ] **File Certificate of Formation**
  - Where: Washington Secretary of State
  - Online: https://ccfs.sos.wa.gov/
  - Cost: **$200 filing fee**
  - Time: 2-3 business days online, 2 weeks by mail
  
- [ ] **Get WA Business License**
  - Where: Washington Dept of Revenue
  - Online: https://dor.wa.gov/
  - Cost: Usually **free** (depends on business type)
  - Required: Within 30 days of registration

### Phase 3: Federal Registration
- [ ] **Get EIN (Employer Identification Number)**
  - Where: IRS
  - Online: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
  - Cost: **FREE**
  - Time: Instant online
  - Need for: Bank account, taxes, hiring

### Phase 4: Operating Agreement
- [ ] **Create LLC Operating Agreement**
  - Not required by WA state but **highly recommended**
  - Defines ownership, management, profit distribution
  - **I can help draft this!**
  - Templates available or hire lawyer ($500-1500)

### Phase 5: Banking & Finances
- [ ] **Open Business Bank Account**
  - Need: EIN, Certificate of Formation, Operating Agreement
  - Options: Chase, Bank of America, US Bank, online banks
  - Keep business and personal finances separate!

- [ ] **Get Business Credit Card** (optional)
  - Helps build business credit
  - Separates expenses

### Phase 6: Compliance
- [ ] **File Annual Report**
  - Due: Annually by renewal date
  - Cost: ~$60/year
  - Washington requires this to stay in good standing

- [ ] **Set up bookkeeping/accounting**
  - Software: QuickBooks, Wave (free), FreshBooks
  - Consider hiring CPA for taxes
