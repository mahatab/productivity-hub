## 💡 Reading Notes & Insights

### Sherlock Holmes - A Scandal in Bohemia
- Irene Adler beats Holmes - the only woman to outwit him
- Holmes values her photograph over emerald ring from King
- Theme: Intelligence and resourcefulness over brute force
- "To Sherlock Holmes she is always THE woman"

### Jane Austen - Pride and Prejudice  
- Opening line is one of literature's most famous
- Mr. Bennet's wit vs Mrs. Bennet's neurosis
- Darcy's pride and initial rudeness sets up transformation
- Elizabeth's "fine eyes" and sharp wit

### Hans Christian Andersen
- Themes: Mortality, vanity, redemption, social class
- Often darker than modern adaptations suggest
- Religious/spiritual undertones in many tales
- Danish folklore influence strong
