# Signal to Noise - Communication Clarity Dashboard
**Date:** March 14, 2026

## 💡 The Problem
We're drowning in messages. Slack, Teams, email, texts, WhatsApp - hundreds of notifications daily. Most are noise. The important stuff gets buried. We miss critical messages because they're hidden in a sea of "👍" reactions and "sounds good!" replies.

## 🎯 The Solution
**Signal to Noise** - An AI-powered dashboard that analyzes ALL your communication channels and surfaces only what truly matters.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Unified Inbox:** One view of Slack, Teams, Email, SMS, WhatsApp
- **AI Priority Scoring:** Machine learning ranks messages by actual importance
  - Action items requiring response
  - Time-sensitive requests
  - Messages from key people (configurable)
  - First-time conversations
- **Smart Filtering:**
  - Auto-hide: reactions only, "thanks", FYIs you're CC'd on
  - Surface: questions, deadlines, decisions needed
- **Focus Mode:** Shows only top 5 most important items
- **Daily Digest:** Morning summary of what you missed
- **Response Tracker:** Reminds you of pending replies

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
Clean, minimal interface. No clutter. Just signal. The opposite of every messaging app that wants your constant attention.

## 👥 Target Users
- Busy professionals drowning in messages
- Managers juggling multiple teams
- Anyone who's missed important messages in the noise

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React + TailwindCSS
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express
- **AI:** OpenAI GPT-4 for message classification
- **Integrations:** Slack API, Microsoft Graph, Gmail API, Twilio
- **Database:** PostgreSQL for message history

## 🎯 M2Labs Fit
Perfect example of "less is more" - cutting through complexity to deliver clarity. Every feature removes friction rather than adding it.

## 💭 Notes
Could start simple with just email + Slack. Add other platforms later. The AI scoring is the key differentiator - truly understanding what matters vs what doesn't.
