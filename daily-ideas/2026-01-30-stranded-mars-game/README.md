# STRANDED - Mars Survival Chat Game

**A text-based narrative game where YOU are the only hope for an astronaut stranded on Mars.**

## 🎮 The Concept

You receive an unexpected message from Dr. Sarah Chen, a NASA astronaut stranded on Mars after her crew evacuated. She's alone, injured, and running out of oxygen. Through a text-based communication system, you must guide her through survival challenges, make critical decisions, and help her stay alive until rescue arrives.

**The twist:** Everything happens in real-time. When she says "I'll check the oxygen tanks in 20 minutes," you actually wait 20 minutes. This creates genuine tension and makes you think about her between sessions.

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Real-time progression** - Game continues even when you're not playing
- **Meaningful choices** - Your decisions have permanent consequences
- **Emotional connection** - She's not just a character, she feels real
- **Scientific accuracy** - Based on real Mars challenges and NASA protocols
- **Asynchronous gameplay** - Perfect for busy people, check in throughout the day
- **Multiple endings** - 6-8 different outcomes based on your choices

## 💡 What Makes It Special

Unlike other text games:
- ✅ **Real stakes** - She can actually die. Game over is permanent.
- ✅ **Genuine connection** - Remembers conversations, builds relationship
- ✅ **No save-scumming** - Decisions are final, just like real life
- ✅ **Educational** - Learn real Mars science while playing
- ✅ **Respects your time** - Async gameplay, no addiction mechanics
