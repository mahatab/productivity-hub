# STRANDED - Mars Survival Chat Game
## Deep Brainstorm & Design Document

**Date:** January 30, 2026  
**Concept:** Text-based narrative game where you help an astronaut stranded on Mars survive through real-time chat

---

## 🎯 CORE CONCEPT

You receive a distress message from Dr. Sarah Chen, a NASA astronaut stranded on Mars after her crew had to evacuate due to a critical system failure. She managed to get to an emergency shelter, but she's alone, injured, and running out of oxygen. Her only link to Earth is you - through a delayed text communication system.

**The Hook:** Real-time progression. When she says "I'll check the oxygen tanks, give me 20 minutes," you actually wait 20 minutes in real life. This creates genuine tension and makes players think about her between sessions.

---

## 📖 NARRATIVE STRUCTURE

### Act 1: The Crisis (Days 1-2)
**Opening:** You receive an unexpected message on your phone/device:
```
UNKNOWN NUMBER
Help. Please. Anyone there?
This is Dr. Sarah Chen, Mars Mission Ares-7
Crew evacuated. I'm alone.
Is anyone receiving this?
```

**Early Goals:**
- Establish trust and connection
- Assess immediate dangers (oxygen, injuries, power)
- Guide her through basic survival tasks
- Learn what went wrong

**Key Moment:** She has 72 hours of oxygen. The clock starts NOW.

### Act 2: Survival (Days 3-7)
**Challenges:**
- Fix oxygen generator (requires finding parts, following technical instructions)
- Treat her injuries with limited medical supplies
- Find food and water
- Deal with psychological stress (loneliness, fear)
- Communication delays (Mars is 8-20 min away, signals take time)

**Branching Paths:**
- Do you prioritize oxygen or her broken arm?
- Risk going outside for supplies or conserve what's in the shelter?
- Should she try to reach the main base (dangerous) or wait for rescue?

**Player Role:**
You're not just giving commands - you're her emotional support, her problem-solver, her hope. She'll ask you about your life, share her fears, make jokes to stay sane.

### Act 3: Hope or Despair (Days 8-14)
**Multiple Endings Based on Your Decisions:**

**Best Ending:** She survives, rescue mission arrives, emotional reunion
**Good Ending:** She survives but with permanent injury/trauma
**Bittersweet Ending:** She survives but loses contact before rescue (uncertain fate)
**Tragic Ending:** Your advice leads to a fatal mistake
**Heroic Ending:** She makes a dangerous trek to the main base and finds a way to signal for rescue

## 🎮 GAME MECHANICS

### Communication System
**Realistic Delays:**
- Messages take 8-20 minutes to reach Mars (depending on orbital position)
- You can't spam advice - each message matters
- Sometimes she'll go offline (sleeping, doing tasks, signal loss)

**Real-Time Progression:**
- Tasks take real time (15 min to check systems, 2 hours to sleep, etc.)
- Game progresses even when you're not playing
- Push notifications: "Sarah needs you" when critical moments happen

**Choice & Consequence:**
- Your advice affects her survival chances
- Multiple decision points with no "right" answer
- Butterfly effect: small early choices impact the ending

### Resource Management (Her Side)
**Player Monitors:**
- Oxygen levels (%)
- Power reserves
- Food/water rations
- Her health status
- Mental state

**Player Can:**
- Give instructions for repairs
- Guide her through medical procedures
- Help her ration resources
- Provide emotional support
- Make critical decisions

### Emotional Connection
**Building the Relationship:**
- She shares stories about her life, family, dreams
- Asks about your day (breaking the 4th wall)
- Sends photos from Mars (beautiful/haunting)
- Voice messages (optional premium feature)
- Her personality evolves based on your interactions

- Not just a character - she feels like a real person

**3. Scientific Accuracy**
- Real Mars facts and challenges

- Believable technology and problems

**4. Asynchronous Gameplay**
- Perfect for busy people
- Check in throughout the day
- Doesn't demand continuous attention
- But rewards staying engaged

## 🎭 CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT

### Dr. Sarah Chen
**Background:**
- Age: 34
- Specialization: Geologist & Engineer
- Personality: Witty, resilient, scientific mind
- Family: Husband and 8-year-old daughter on Earth
- Motivation: See her family again

**Character Arc:**
- **Day 1-2:** Panic → Determination
- **Day 3-5:** Hope → Doubt
- **Day 6-8:** Breaking point OR renewed strength (player dependent)
- **Day 9-14:** Acceptance OR triumph

**Her Voice:**
- Uses humor to cope
- Gets philosophical late at night
- Shows vulnerability but fights to stay strong
- References pop culture (movies, music)
- Talks about food she misses

**Evolution Based on Player:**
- Supportive player → She becomes hopeful and trusting
- Harsh player → She becomes desperate and reckless
- Neglectful player → She loses hope, gives up

## 🛠️ TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION

### Core Systems

**1. Message Queue System**
```
Player sends message
↓
Delay calculation (8-20 min based on Mars position)
↓
Sarah's AI processes message
↓
Response generated based on:
  - Current situation
  - Resource levels
  - Previous conversations
  - Player relationship score
  - Random events
↓
Delay again
↓
Player receives response
```

**2. Resource Simulation**
- Runs in background
- Oxygen depletes at realistic rate
- Power consumption based on activities
- Food/water consumption
- All values update in real-time

**3. Event System**
- Random events (dust storms, equipment failure)
- Scheduled events (rescue window, supply caches)
- Player-triggered events (decisions leading to consequences)

**4. AI Conversation**
- Context-aware responses
- Remembers player's advice
- Personality consistency
- Emotional state tracking

**5. Save System**
- Cloud saves (cross-device)
- Can't reload to change decisions
- Progress checkpoints (for technical issues only)

### Tech Stack

**Prototype/MVP:**
- HTML/CSS/JavaScript (web app)
- LocalStorage for saves
- Simple rule-based conversation

**Production:**
- React Native (iOS/Android apps)
- Node.js backend
- Firebase for real-time sync
- AI (GPT/Claude) for dynamic conversations
- Push notification system
- Analytics for player choices
