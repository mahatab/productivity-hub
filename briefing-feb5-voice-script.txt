M 2 LABS IDEA OF THE DAY: Task Batch Processor.

The concept: Most productivity apps interrupt you constantly with notifications for individual tasks. What if instead, you could batch-process your tasks in focused 30-minute windows? The app would group similar tasks together - all phone calls at once, all emails at once, all creative work at once - and present them in optimized batches. This reduces context switching, the biggest productivity killer. Features would include: AI-powered task clustering, customizable batch windows, distraction blocking during batches, and analytics on your batch completion rates.
