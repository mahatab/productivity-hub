💡 NEW M2LABS IDEA

📺 StreamZen - The Anti-Streaming App

The Problem: Ever opened Netflix, scrolled for 30 minutes, and ended up watching nothing? Or subscribed to 5 streaming services and still can't find what you want? Streaming has become exhausting.

The Solution: StreamZen is the opposite of every streaming app. Instead of infinite scrolling and algorithmic recommendations, it shows you exactly 3 things:

1. What you started - Resume your in-progress shows/movies
2. What's worth watching tonight - One curated pick based on your mood (quick survey: "Funny? Thrilling? Thoughtful?")
3. The button - "I don't know, surprise me" that picks something good from your services

Features:
→ Aggregates Netflix, Prime, HBO, Disney+, etc. into one clean interface
→ No endless scrolling - max 3 recommendations at a time
→ No autoplay trailers - just title, rating, runtime, and one-sentence description
→ "Watch Later" list limited to 10 items (forces prioritization)
→ Weekly digest: "You spent 2 hours scrolling last week. Here's what you actually watched: 0 hours."

M2Labs Philosophy: Less choice = more enjoyment. Decision fatigue is the enemy of entertainment.
