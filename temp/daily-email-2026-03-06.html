💡 NEW APP IDEA: FocusFlow

🎯 The Problem

We're drowning in notifications all day long. Every ping breaks our focus, kills productivity, and drains mental energy. Most notification settings are all-or-nothing, and "Do Not Disturb" is too blunt an instrument.

✨ The Concept

FocusFlow is an AI-powered notification manager that learns your work patterns and intelligently filters notifications based on context, urgency, and your current focus state.

🔧 Key Features

  • Smart Learning: AI learns which notifications actually matter to you and when
  • Context Awareness: Knows if you're in a meeting, coding, or free
  • Intelligent Grouping: Batches similar notifications for scheduled delivery
  • VIP Breakthrough: Certain people/apps always get through
  • Focus Zones: Different rules for work, home, gym, driving
  • Smart Summaries: "Here's what you missed during focus time" digest
  • Calendar Integration: Auto-adjusts based on your schedule

🎨 The m2labs Philosophy

Perfectly aligned with m2labs' mission: take a chaotic, overwhelming system (notifications) and simplify it with focused, intelligent features. Less noise, more clarity, deeper focus.

👥 Target Users

Knowledge workers, developers, executives, students - anyone who needs deep focus but can't afford to miss critical updates.

⚙️ Tech Stack

iOS/Android native apps, on-device ML for privacy, calendar API integration, notification interception, analytics dashboard.
